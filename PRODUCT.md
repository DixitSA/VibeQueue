# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Patrons in a taproom/venue environment who need a low-friction way to request and upvote music. Venue owners managing the session flow and moderation.

## Product Purpose
A zero-hardware, real-time crowd jukebox. It exists to turn listeners into participants, enhancing the venue atmosphere while maintaining a premium, "Monocle-magazine" aesthetic.

## Brand Personality
Editorial, Precise, Tactile. The interface feels like a high-end physical menu or a modern digital tool (Linear-level efficiency) with Apple Music-level typographic elegance.

## Anti-references
- Neon/Cyberpunk cliches.
- Default web blue/green/purple palettes.
- SaaS cliches (hero-metric templates, cluttered card grids).
- Cluttered, multi-column layouts on mobile.

## Design Principles
- **Tactile Precision**: Every interaction (like the card flip) should feel physically grounded and deliberate.
- **Editorial Calm**: High-contrast, clean typography that remains readable in low-light environments without being overstimulating.
- **One-Handed Utility**: Optimized for one-handed thumb navigation in a social setting (large targets, bottom-heavy actions).
- **Zero-Friction Flow**: Anonymous, instant access via QR code with minimal cognitive load.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast (Charcoal/Cream) for low-light taproom readability.
- Reduced motion consideration for the 3D card flip (ensure accessibility preferences are respected).
- Large tap targets for social/distracted environments.
